**Why Focus on Tones?**

Vietnamese is a tonal language where the same syllable can have six different meanings depending on its tone. Many learners struggle because they try to learn vocabulary without first training their ears to hear tonal differences.

Vietnamese Tone Trainer takes a different approach: master the tones first, and vocabulary acquisition becomes much easier.
